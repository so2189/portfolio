    Overview

    Paw Pals is a charming single-player cat shelter simulator where players take on the role of a shelter manager. Complete daily tasks, care for cats, and earn points as the story unfolds in a calm and immersive gameplay experience designed to be both relaxing and engaging.

    Features

    • ✦ Peaceful, single-player cat shelter simulator
    • ✦ Daily tasks and mini-goals with point collection
    • ✦ Storyline progression with unlockable events
    • ✦ Custom sprite sheets and artwork for cats, environments, and UI
    • ✦ Point system tracking progress and rewards
    • ✦ Audio effects and background music for immersion
    • ✦ Intuitive page flow and gameplay mechanics

    Tech Stack

    • ✦  GML
    • ✦  Sprite Sheets & Artwork
    • ✦  Audio & Music Integration
