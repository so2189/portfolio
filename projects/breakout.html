    • Breakout

    A simple arcade-style breakout game where the player controls a paddle to bounce a ball that collects treasures flying across the screen. The ball speeds up with each bounce and the goal is to collect as many treasures as possible before missing the ball.

    Visit Site

    Overview

    In Breakout, you control a paddle at the bottom of the screen using the A and D keys. A ball bounces around, speeding up each time it hits the paddle or walls. Two treasures move horizontally across the screen, and your objective is to bounce the ball to collect these treasures for points.

    Gameplay Details

    • Use A and D keys to move the paddle left and right; paddle stops at screen edges.
    • The ball starts stationary at the top middle and launches on mouse click with random speed.
    • Ball bounces off the paddle, walls, and top border, increasing speed slightly on each bounce.
    • Missing the ball resets the game and score.
    • Two treasures move left-to-right and right-to-left at random vertical positions for the ball to collect.
    • Score is displayed live at the top left corner of the canvas.

    Technical Features

    • Keyboard controls with paddle constrained within screen borders.
    • Dynamic ball speed and bounce logic, including bounce angle depending on paddle hit position.
    • Gradual ball color cycling effect for visual appeal.
    • Seamless parallax background with foreground and background layers moving at different speeds.
    • Sound effects for ball bounces, treasure collection, and ball loss.
